# Problem Statement

## Background

Sports injuries are a major concern for athletes, coaches, and sports organizations. Many injuries occur due to improper movement techniques, poor posture, muscle fatigue, excessive training load, and repetitive stress. These injuries can negatively impact an athlete's performance, career, and overall health.

Traditional injury assessment methods rely on manual observation by coaches or physiotherapists. Although effective in many cases, these methods are time-consuming, subjective, and often detect injuries only after they have occurred.

With advancements in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision, it is now possible to analyze athlete movements automatically and identify biomechanical abnormalities before they lead to serious injuries.

---

## Existing System

Current injury assessment methods mainly depend on:

- Manual observation by coaches.
- Video analysis performed manually.
- Periodic physical assessments.
- Rehabilitation after an injury occurs.

These approaches focus more on treatment than prevention.

---

## Limitations of Existing Systems

The existing systems have several limitations:

- Manual analysis is time-consuming.
- Results depend on the experience of the observer.
- Continuous monitoring is difficult.
- Early detection of injury risk is limited.
- No automated biomechanical analysis.
- Lack of personalized injury risk prediction.
- Limited data-driven decision-making.

---

## Proposed Solution

The proposed system is an AI-powered Sports Injury Risk Detection Platform that automatically analyzes athlete movement videos using pose estimation and biomechanical analysis.

The system will:

- Allow athletes to upload movement videos.
- Detect body keypoints using pose estimation.
- Analyze movement patterns and posture.
- Identify biomechanical abnormalities.
- Predict the probability of sports injuries.
- Generate injury risk scores.
- Recommend corrective exercises and preventive actions.
- Provide dashboards for athletes, coaches, physiotherapists, and sports scientists.

---

## Benefits of the Proposed System

The proposed solution provides the following advantages:

- Early detection of injury risks.
- Improved athlete safety.
- Better movement analysis using AI.
- Reduced dependence on manual observation.
- Personalized recommendations.
- Continuous athlete performance monitoring.
- Data-driven coaching and rehabilitation support.

---

## Conclusion

The proposed AI Sports Injury Risk Detection Platform aims to shift injury management from a reactive approach to a proactive approach. By combining Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Biomechanical Analysis, the system can help athletes improve performance while minimizing the risk of injuries.
