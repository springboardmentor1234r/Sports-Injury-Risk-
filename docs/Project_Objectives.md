# Project Objectives

## Primary Objective

To develop an AI-powered Sports Injury Risk Detection Platform that analyzes athlete movement videos using Computer Vision, Pose Estimation, and Biomechanical Analysis to identify abnormal movement patterns, predict potential sports injuries, and provide preventive recommendations to improve athlete safety and performance.

---

## Specific Objectives

The project aims to achieve the following objectives:

### 1. Secure User Management

- Develop a secure user authentication system.
- Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- Support multiple user roles such as Athlete, Coach, Physiotherapist, Sports Scientist, and Administrator.

---

### 2. Athlete Profile Management

- Store athlete personal information.
- Maintain injury history records.
- Record training profiles.
- Track athlete performance data.
- Manage physical assessment records.

---

### 3. Video Management

- Allow athletes to upload movement videos.
- Validate uploaded videos.
- Preprocess videos for AI analysis.
- Extract video frames for pose estimation.

---

### 4. Pose Estimation

- Detect human body keypoints.
- Generate body skeletons.
- Track joint movements.
- Analyze body posture during sports activities.

---

### 5. Biomechanical Analysis

- Calculate joint angles.
- Measure body symmetry.
- Analyze posture quality.
- Detect abnormal movement patterns.

---

### 6. Injury Risk Prediction

- Identify injury risk factors.
- Predict possible sports injuries.
- Calculate injury probability.
- Monitor injury risk trends over time.

---

### 7. Recommendation System

- Suggest corrective exercises.
- Recommend mobility improvements.
- Provide recovery guidance.
- Recommend training modifications.

---

### 8. Dashboard and Analytics

- Display athlete performance metrics.
- Visualize injury risk scores.
- Generate movement analysis reports.
- Provide analytics for coaches and physiotherapists.

---

### 9. Reporting System

- Generate injury assessment reports.
- Export reports in PDF and Excel formats.
- Maintain athlete assessment history.

---

## Expected Outcomes

By the completion of this project, the system will:

- Reduce the likelihood of sports injuries through early risk detection.
- Improve athlete performance using AI-driven movement analysis.
- Support coaches and physiotherapists with objective performance data.
- Provide intelligent recommendations for injury prevention.
- Enable continuous monitoring of athlete health and performance.
